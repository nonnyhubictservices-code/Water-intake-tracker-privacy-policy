Privacy Policy

Water Intake Tracker respects your privacy.

This application does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All hydration data entered into the app is stored locally on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personal information
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos or media files
  • Financial information
  • Device tracking information

Data Storage

  • Water intake records are stored only on your device.
  • No data is transmitted over the internet.
  • No account registration or login is required.
  • Users have full control over their stored data.

Third-Party Services

Water Intake Tracker does not use analytics services, advertising networks, or third-party tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for users of all ages and does not knowingly collect, use, or disclose personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app listing and within this document.
